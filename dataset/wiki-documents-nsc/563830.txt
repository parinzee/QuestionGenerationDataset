Fortaleza is in the process of converting its suburban rail network into a full metro system. See wiki entry for the Fortaleza Metro. 2. Sydney, Melbourne, Copenhagen, London, and Paris (RER) have hybrid systems, with trains running as metro-like services (on segregated track—usually in tunnel—and with high frequency) in their inner cities, but as main line commuter services in the suburbs.</doc>
