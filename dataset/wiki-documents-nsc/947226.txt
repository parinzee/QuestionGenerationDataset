The second runner-up of each edition of Miss Universe is the third placer behind the candidate who is crowned as Miss Universe and the first runner-up (second placer). Although it has never happened, the second runner-up is supposed to take over the Miss Universe title if both the original winner and the first runner-up are unable to fulfill their duties/resign their titles. For example, if a Miss Universe winner is required to give up her title and the first runner-up is unable/does not want to assume the winner's position, the title then passes to the second runner-up.
