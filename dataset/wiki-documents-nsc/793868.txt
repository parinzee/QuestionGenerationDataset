(1949) - Nights on the Road (1952) - The Devil Makes Three (1952) - Homesick for You (1952) - Jonny Saves Nebrador (1953) - Salto Mortale (1953) - It Was Always So Nice With You (1954) - Murder Party (1961)</doc>
