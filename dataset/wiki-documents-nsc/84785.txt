(1981) - The Ballad of Narayama (1983, Palme d'Or) - Zegen (1987) - Black Rain (1989) - The Eel (1998, Palme d'Or) - Dr. Akagi (1998) - Warm Water Under a Red Bridge (2001) - 11'901 September 11" (2002)</doc>
